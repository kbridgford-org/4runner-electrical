## Getting Started

**WARNING:** See the Important Safety and Product Information guide in the product box for product warnings and other important information.

**CAUTION:** Garmin strongly recommends having an experienced installer with proper knowledge of electrical systems install the device. Incorrectly wiring the power cables can result in damage to the device, the vehicle, or the battery, and can cause bodily injury.

**NOTICE:** Disconnect the negative battery terminal in the vehicle before installing the device to prevent an electrical short circuit. Reconnect the vehicle battery terminals only after installation is complete.

1. **Mount the device** (page 2).
2. **Connect accessories** to the device (page 5).
3. **Connect a control input** (optional) (page 6).
4. **Connect the device to vehicle power** (page 7).
5. **Pair your device** with a compatible Garmin navigation device or a smartphone (page 8).

## Installation

### Mounting Considerations

* The device must be mounted close to the battery and within range of the supplied power cable.
* The mounting surface must be strong enough to support the device's weight.
* The location must allow room for routing and connecting all cables.



### Mounting the Device

**NOTICE:** Avoid wires, gauges, air bags, air bag covers, HVAC, and other items when drilling pilot holes and attaching the mount to the vehicle.

1. Place the mount on the selected location.
2. Using the mount as a template, mark the two screw locations.
3. Drill pilot holes if necessary for the mounting material (do not drill through the mount).
4. Securely fasten the mount to the surface using appropriate hardware.

### Opening the Front Cover

You must open the front cover to make electrical connections.
1. Lift the front cover latch and rotate it counter-clockwise.
2. Pull the front cover off of the device.



### Connecting Accessories

**NOTICE:** * **Do not** use the device to control or power a winch.
**Do not** connect a power source to an accessory output terminal.
**Do not** connect accessory ground wires to the GND terminal on the Garmin PowerSwitch device.

**Accessory Requirements:**

**Up to 30 A** per channel; maximum **100 A** for the entire system.


**Wire Gauges:**
**20 to 30 A:** 10 AWG ().
**10 to 20 A:** 12 AWG ().
**Less than 10 A:** 14 AWG ().


**Steps:**

1. Connect the positive wire from the accessory to one of the accessory terminals.
2. Connect the negative/ground wire to an unpainted ground stud on the vehicle frame, a distribution block, or the negative battery terminal.
3. Verify all connections are tight.

## Connecting to Vehicle Power

1. Connect the large ring end of the included ground wire to the **negative** battery terminal.
2. Connect the small ring end of the ground wire to the **GND terminal** (2) on the device.
3. Connect the small ring end of the included 12V cable to the **+12V terminal** (3) on the device.
4. Tighten the  in. nut on the +12V terminal to a maximum torque of **4.52 N-m (40 lbf-in.)**. **Do not overtighten**.
5. Connect the large ring end of the 12V cable to the **positive** battery terminal.

## Garmin PowerSwitch App

The app enables wireless digital switching from your smartphone or navigation device.

|Number|App Feature|Description|
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Settings | Opens the app settings menu |
| **2** | Edit | Enters editing mode to customize layout and functions |
| **3** | Off | Turns off all outputs |
| **4** | Power buttons | Activates each connected output |
| **5** | Voltage | Displays input voltage |
| **6** | Amperage | Displays total system amperage |
| **7** | Device Name | Displays the device name |
| **8** | Tabs | Displays device tabs and custom layout tabs |

### Pairing

**Navigation Device:** Open the Garmin PowerSwitch app on the device and follow on-screen instructions while within 1 m (3.3 ft.) of the powered switch.
**Smartphone:** Install the Garmin PowerSwitch app, open it, and follow instructions while within 1 m (3.3 ft.) of the powered switch.
**Additional Devices:** You can pair up to four PowerSwitch devices.

### Troubleshooting

**Not staying connected:** Verify power, check that Bluetooth is enabled, move closer to the device, and ensure the vehicle battery is at least 12 V.
**No power to accessories:** The device automatically turns off all outputs if the battery falls below 11 V to preserve power. Outputs turn back on when power exceeds 12 V.
