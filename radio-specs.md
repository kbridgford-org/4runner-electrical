
## Antenna Install Location
The Antenna will be installed on a ditch light bracket on the passenger side.

## LMR-195 Antenna Cable

The LMR-195 coaxial cable has a standardized outside diameter across its various versions (Standard, UltraFlex, and Direct Burial).
Outside Diameter (OD) Summary

Decimal Inch: 0.195 in.
Metric: 4.953 mm (often rounded to 4.95 mm).
